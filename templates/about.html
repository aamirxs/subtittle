About Subtitle Generator

Welcome to Subtitle Generator, an advanced web application designed to make video subtitle generation easy and accessible. Our platform leverages state-of-the-art AI technology to provide accurate transcription and subtitle generation in multiple languages.

Multiple Languages

Support for 12+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean.

Multiple Formats

Export subtitles in popular formats including SRT, WebVTT, and Advanced SubStation Alpha (ASS) for maximum compatibility.

Wide Format Support

Support for various video formats including MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, and WebM, with a generous 2GB file size limit.

Built with modern web technologies and powered by the Whisper AI model, our platform ensures high accuracy and reliability in subtitle generation. Whether you're a content creator, educator, or just need subtitles for your videos, we've got you covered.
